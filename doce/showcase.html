Product Overview

A closer look at the Black Hole V1.0 device and its capabilities.

Compact & Powerful Design

The Black Hole V1.0 combines powerful capabilities in a compact form factor. With its dual-band support and external antennas, it provides exceptional range and performance for WiFi security testing.

The device features a durable ABS plastic case with convenient access to all ports and buttons, making it perfect for both field use and lab testing.

Advanced Connectivity

With dual SMA antenna connectors, the Black Hole V1.0 offers superior signal reception and transmission capabilities. The included 3dBi dual-band antennas provide excellent coverage, while the option to upgrade to high-gain directional antennas gives you flexibility for different testing scenarios.

The device also features USB-C connectivity for charging, firmware updates, and direct computer connections.

Technical Specifications

Detailed hardware and software specifications of the Black Hole V1.0 device.

Processor & Memory

  • Main Processor: ESP32 dual-core @ 240MHz
  • Co-processor: BW16 for enhanced wireless
  • RAM: 520KB SRAM
  • Flash Memory: 4MB

Wireless Capabilities

  • WiFi: 802.11 b/g/n/ac (2.4GHz + 5GHz)
  • Bluetooth: BLE 5.0
  • Antenna Connectors: 2× SMA
  • TX Power: Up to 20dBm (adjustable)

Power & Battery

  • Battery: 1200mAh LiPo (replaceable)
  • Runtime: 4-6 hours (typical usage)
  • Charging: USB-C, 5V/1A
  • Charge Time: Approximately 2-3 hours

Physical Specifications

  • Dimensions: 80mm × 50mm × 15mm
  • Weight: 60g (with battery)
  • Operating Temperature: 0°C to 40°C
  • Case Material: ABS plastic

Use Cases

Educational and security testing scenarios where Black Hole excels.

Educational Environments

Black Hole V1.0 is perfect for classroom settings, workshops, and training programs focused on network security. Instructors can use the device to demonstrate WiFi security concepts, vulnerability assessment techniques, and proper security practices.

The comprehensive documentation and educational resources make it easy to integrate into existing cybersecurity curricula.

Security Testing

Security professionals can use Black Hole V1.0 to perform authorized security assessments of their own networks or networks they have permission to test. The device helps identify potential vulnerabilities, misconfigurations, and security weaknesses.

With its portable design and battery power, Black Hole is ideal for field testing and security audits in various environments.
