Black Hole Features

Explore the powerful capabilities of the Black Hole WiFi security testing device.

Core Features

Black Hole V1.0 combines cutting-edge hardware with versatile software to deliver a comprehensive WiFi security testing platform.

Dual-Band Deauth

Supports both 2.4GHz and 5GHz frequency bands, allowing for comprehensive testing across all modern WiFi networks.

ESP32 + BW16 Chipset

Powerful hardware combination providing exceptional performance, reliability, and advanced wireless capabilities.

Onboard LiPo Charging

Built-in battery charging circuit for portable operation, with intelligent power management for extended usage time.

SMA External Antennas

Standard SMA connectors for the included antennas, with support for upgrading to high-gain or directional antennas.

Power + Reset Buttons

Dedicated hardware buttons for easy operation and quick recovery in case of software issues.

Flashable Firmware

Easily update or customize your device with our open-source firmware options and regular updates.

Web Interface Access

Intuitive browser-based control panel accessible from any device, with no additional software required.

Custom Case Ready

Designed to fit standard 3D-printed cases, with mounting points for easy installation and customization.

Educational Use Only

Created specifically for educational purposes and ethical security testing, with comprehensive documentation.

Technical Specifications

Detailed hardware specifications of the Black Hole V1.0 device.

Hardware Specifications

  • Main Processor: ESP32 dual-core @ 240MHz
  • Co-processor: BW16 for enhanced wireless capabilities
  • RAM: 520KB SRAM
  • Flash Memory: 4MB
  • WiFi: 802.11 b/g/n/ac (2.4GHz + 5GHz)
  • Bluetooth: BLE 5.0
  • Battery: 1200mAh LiPo (replaceable)
  • Dimensions: 80mm × 50mm × 15mm (without antennas)

Important information about the responsible use of Black Hole.

Black Hole is designed for educational purposes and security testing on networks you own or have explicit permission to test. Always use this device responsibly and ethically.

Unauthorized testing on third-party networks may be illegal in your jurisdiction. Always check local laws and regulations before use.
